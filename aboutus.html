Our Mission

Together's mission is to nourish communities and inspire generosity through innovative food donation. We strive to create a world where no one goes hungry, by connecting those who can give with those in need.

What Do We Do?

We leverage technology to make food donation as easy as ordering food. Our platform allows individuals and businesses to donate food through local catering services, directly to those most in need. We believe in the power of shared food to build stronger, more connected communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 in Surabaya, Together started with a simple idea: what if donating food was as easy as ordering food? Our founders saw an opportunity to use technology to bridge the gap between surplus food and hunger in society.
